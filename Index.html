    Asus week's continued the evolution

    I have always liked Asus’ TUF (“The Ultimate Force”) brand for its simplistic design and focus on performance over the glowing LEDs and edge externals. They don’t go with my personality, even if I am a gamer. Anyway, whether you like a design or not is a personal choice.TUF Gaming F15 gives the vibe of gaming notebooks when I started my career as a tech journalist and began reviewing laptops.Old school yet modern and impressive to look at, the 2.3-kg device is light enough to carry around in a backpack but its dimensions are also thick.It meets military spec MIL-STD durability standards but isn’t boring to look at. The laptop has hard edges and prominent TUF branding on the back, which looks really nice. The notebook comes in a grey finish, and the bottom case has a smooth, texture to the right and left of the keyboard as well as a honeycomb pattern at the bottom of the chassis.The TUF Gaming F15 has just the right selection of ports too: three USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type-A ports, headset and Ethernet jacks, a full-size HDMI 2.0b video output (which is fine for 4K on an external monitor at 60Hz) and the Thunderbolt 4/USB-C port. There’s also Bluetooth 5.0 and Wi-Fi 6 support, and a 720 webcam plus microphone.


    Windows 10 users gets Windows 11 Updates . Check if you got yours

    Thanks to the Windows 11 Insider Preview ISOs, users will be able to download a preview of the upcoming update and install it on a new computer or format an old one and give it a fresh start - if it is compatible. This can also come in handy for users who don’t have a good internet connection and need an “offline” installation method that can be used to install Windows 11 on their computer. If you have a valid Windows 10 licence, you should be able to download the Windows 11 update by visiting the company’s website and signing up for the Insider program. It is safer to pick the Beta channel over the Dev version if you prefer stability. You can then download the ISO file which can be used just like a Windows 10 ISO.
